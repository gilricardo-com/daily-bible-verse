# Privacy Policy for Daily Bible Verse

Last updated: November 26, 2024

## Data Collection
Daily Bible Verse does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

## Local Storage
The app stores data locally on your device only:
- Favorite verses you choose to save
- Downloaded Bible books for offline reading
- Your app preferences (theme, language)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

## Third-Party Services
The app uses the bolls.life API to retrieve Bible verses. No personal information is sent to this service.
